About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Manchester, advised by Prof. Sophia Ananiadou. Prior to that, I graduated from Virginia Polytechnic Institute & State University (Virginia Tech) with a M.S. in Computer Science, in which I was advised by Prof. Hoda Eldardiry. I obtained my B.S. in Electronic Information Science and Technology from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.

My research covers a range of topics in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. For now, I'm focusing on knowledge graph and related applications. (Check my CV/RESUME here).
